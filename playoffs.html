Article: My 2025 NBA Playoffs Second Round Predictions

Want some second round playoff predictions? In this article, I preview each second round matchup and give my series prediction along with a detailed explanation.

Current Playoff Picture

  • Eastern Conference Finals: Celtics vs. Heat
  • Western Conference Finals: Nuggets vs. Lakers

Series Predictions

  • Celtics vs. Heat: Celtics in 6
  • Nuggets vs. Lakers: Nuggets in 7

The Nuggets have the best player in the series (Jokic), but the Lakers have the pedigree and LeBron's playoff experience.

Players to Watch

  • Anthony Davis - When healthy, he's the best two-way big in the game
