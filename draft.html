Article: My Top 5 Prospects In The 2025 NBA Draft

Check out my article of the Top 5 Prospects in the 2025 NBA Draft, with stats, a full scouting report, and NBA comparisons for each prospect!

Top 5 Draft Prospects

  1. Victor Wembanyama - The clear #1 pick with generational talent
  2. Scoot Henderson - Explosive guard with superstar potential
  3. Brandon Miller - Smooth shooting wing with great size
  4. Amen Thompson - Elite athlete with playmaking skills
  5. Ausar Thompson - Two-way wing with tremendous upside

Sleepers to Watch

  • Jordan Hawkins - Sharpshooter who could be this year's Desmond Bane
  • Leonard Miller - Raw but talented forward with huge upside
  • Rayan Rupert - International wing with defensive potential

Team Needs Analysis

The Pistons desperately need shooting, while the Rockets could use a true point guard. The Spurs will likely build around Wembanyama if they get the #1 pick.
