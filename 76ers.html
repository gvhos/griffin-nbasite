Philadelphia 76ers Coverage

My bold (and sometimes not so bold) predictions about the NBA season and future.

Season Awards Predictions

  • MVP: Nikola Jokic - The Joker will three-peat
  • Rookie of the Year: Paolo Banchero - Immediate impact player
  • Defensive Player of the Year: Evan Mobley - The next great defensive big
  • Most Improved: Tyrese Haliburton - Breakout season in Indiana
  • Sixth Man: Malcolm Brogdon - Perfect role on a contender

Championship Predictions

I'm predicting the Milwaukee Bucks to win it all this year. Giannis is still in his prime, Jrue Holiday provides elite defense, and if Khris Middleton stays healthy, they have the most complete team in the league.

Long-Term Predictions

  • Victor Wembanyama will win MVP within his first 5 seasons
  • The NBA will expand to Seattle and Las Vegas by 2025
  • Load management rules will significantly change the regular season structure
  • The three-point revolution will peak and we'll see a return to more balanced offenses
